How it works

Simple. Automatic. Invisible.

The clinic doesn't change anything. The patient doesn't need a wallet. Everything happens on-chain, automatically.

1

Clinic registers

The receptionist enters the patient's ID and the actual attendance time.

2

Smart contract calculates

HORMI automatically calculates the delay and mints points on Avalanche.

3

Patient gets notified

"Your time has value — here are 150 HORMI Points." Push notification, instant.

4

Patient redeems

Coffee, pharmacy, psychology, gym — real benefits at partner businesses.

5

Protocol earns

3% fee on every redemption, automatically deducted on-chain. No middlemen.

Technology

Built for the real world
